How it works

Jobs come to you.

No cold calling, no marketing, no chasing leads. ScuffIQ handles everything before and after the repair.

Step 1

Customer submits a quote

A customer uploads damage photos on scuffiq.com. Our AI assesses the damage and generates an estimate instantly.

Step 2

You receive the job

We send you a secure review link by email with the customer details, photos, AI estimate and location — everything you need to decide.

Step 3

You confirm your price

Review the job and confirm your price and availability. Takes under 2 minutes. We handle the customer communication from there.

Step 4

Show up and get paid

The customer books a slot in your calendar. You carry out the repair, collect payment and move on to the next job.

Requirements

What we need from you.

ScuffIQ technicians operate as independent sole traders. Here's exactly what you need before you can start taking jobs.

🚐

Your own van

You must have your own vehicle to travel to customer locations. A van is preferred to carry equipment.

📋

Sole trader / self-employed

You must be registered as self-employed or a sole trader with HMRC. ScuffIQ does not employ technicians directly.

🛡️

Public liability insurance

You must hold a minimum of £1 million public liability insurance. We will ask for proof before your first job.

You also need

Proven SMART repair experience (scratch, scuff, alloy or bumper)
Your own professional tools and equipment
A smartphone to receive and respond to job notifications
The ability to respond to jobs promptly — same day preferred
A professional attitude and high standard of finish

You don't need

Formal qualifications or certificates — experience counts
To find your own customers — we bring the jobs to you
To handle invoicing, admin or chasing payment
To commit to fixed hours — work when you choose
To pay any joining fee or upfront costs — ever
⚠️

Important: ScuffIQ technicians are self-employed independent contractors, not employees. You are responsible for your own tax, National Insurance, insurance and equipment. By applying you confirm you understand and accept these terms.
